444
Project

Detection and Prediction of Communities in Friendster

UMJI-SJTU VE444 Course Project

Keywords: Community Detection, Data Analysis, Networks

Community detection has received a great interest as it can be used to reveal useful information regarding a network. For instance, in social networks, it can reveal group of individuals with mutual interests. There are numerous algorithms that have been proposed to perform community detection within networks with varying performance. However, the performance of these algorithms deteriorate as the structure of networks become more complex and the fraction of the neighbor of nodes belonging to different communities than that of the benchmarking node increases. Thus, choosing the proper algorithm to give a reliable community detection result is a tricky matter. In this paper, we explored several community detection algorithms and implemented the central node based link prediction(CLPE) method in Python to detect communities within Friendster.

Reverse Polish-Notation Calculator

UMJI-SJTU VE280 Course Project

Keywords: RPN calculator, C++

An RPN calculator is one in which the operators appear after their respective operands, rather than between them. So, instead of computing the following: (2 + 3) * 5, an RPN calculator would compute this equivalent expressionas 2 3 + 5 *. To print result, enter "p". Each input are pushed to the top of the stacks, enter "d" to duplicate the item at the top of the stack. To negate the item at the top of the stack, enter "n". To clear the stack, enter "c". To print all items on the stack, enter "a". To quit the program, enter "q".
Here is an examplar input for 2 * (3 + 4):
